## $cc, Caption Contest
AI generated random new yorker style cartoon. Suggest a caption and ppl vote for the best

## $ec, Exquisite Corpse
no winners in this one. you just take turns writing sentences to build a story, but you can only see the last 4 words of the previous one.

## $fg, Fruit Guesser

## $mg, Movie Guesser

## $mw or $wordle, Multiplayer Wordle

## $sh, Spell Hell
take turns adding one letter to a growing word fragment — complete a real word (of minimum length) and you lose a life. You can also challenge people if you think they aren't headed towards a real word. Hard game!

## $tr, Trivia For Retards
fill-in-the-blank trivia. $tr for 100 questions, $tr [number] for custom count. $trtime [seconds] to change question time.

## $tt, Two Truths & A Lie

## $wg, Word Guesser
a definition of a somewhat common word is shown and first to guess the word wins.

## $wr, Word Rush
